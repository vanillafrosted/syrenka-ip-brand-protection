## Editorial Principles to Apply

All Scene 1 questions will follow the established Editorial Handbook standards:

**From Scene Bible:**
- Organize by emotional truth, not clock time
- Reality before story (no invention)
- Point of discovery (readers discover when Ray discovers)
- Camera position stays close to Ray
- Present tense, immediate moment
- Readers walk beside Ray (not as Ray)
- Protect ordinary moments
- Small details matter
- Preserve uncertainty over invention
- Never move facts in time

**From Editorial Manifesto:**
- Ray is a real person (not invented character)
- Authenticity over drama
- Never invent to fill gaps
- Ray learns while acting (not an expert)
