## Product Image Protection - What Ray Discovered

**Level 1: Basic Protection (emerged from first attempts)**
- Adding logo/trademark to images
- Consistent visual design
- Makes images obviously belonging to her

**Level 2: Stronger Protection (discovered after more experience)**
- Own photography
- Creative design choices
- Custom graphics
- Copyright transfer agreements if outsourcing

**Level 3: Maximum Protection (emerged over time)**
- Including her own image/face
- Personal story connection
- Makes copying extremely difficult

**Important:** These weren't a premeditated framework. Ray discovered them through trial and error. She was solving problems as they arose.
