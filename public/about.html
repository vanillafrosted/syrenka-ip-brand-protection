Our Mission

To empower entrepreneurs and small business owners worldwide to build and protect their brands with practical, experience-based strategies that actually work.

Our Story

Syrenka was born from real-world experiences protecting and growing brands across borders. After handling 36+ brand protection cases—from trademark disputes to counterfeit product wars on global marketplaces—we realized something important:

Most entrepreneurs don't need complex legal jargon. They need practical guidance. They need someone who understands the struggle, the fear, and the confusion of watching your brand be copied or stolen.

That's what Syrenka is: a friendly expert sharing real strategies from real cases. Not theory. Not assumptions. Just practical knowledge that works.
